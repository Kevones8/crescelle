Everything, one tab

Instead of a lofi video in one tab, a pomodoro site in another and sticky notes somewhere else — Crescelle puts your whole ambient workspace on the page you open most.

Your GIFs, alive

Point Crescelle at a folder and it becomes a smart slideshow: short GIFs loop until they've had their moment, long ones play once or twice — you decide. No distortion, ever.

Your soundtrack

Play your own music folder with full controls, shuffle and volume. It resumes exactly where you left off — same song, same second. Spotify support coming.

Focus widgets

Pomodoro and timers that keep counting even when the tab is closed, plus clock, weather, to-do list, quick links, countdowns and word of the day — each one floats wherever you want it.

Sticky notes

Unlimited notes in soft colors. Drag them anywhere, hide one or hide them all, and they're still there tomorrow.

Picture-in-picture

Float your wall above every other window — with its own mini controls for images and music — while you work in anything else.

Google Drive sync

Premium: stream images and music straight from a Drive folder, and your setup follows you to any computer you sign into.

Simple pricing
